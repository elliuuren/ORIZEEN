Corrections Policy

Last updated: 2026

Our Commitment

ORIZEEN Times is committed to accuracy. When we make mistakes, we correct them promptly and transparently.

How We Handle Corrections

When a factual error is identified, we update the article and add a correction note at the bottom indicating what was changed and when. We do not silently edit articles.

Reporting an Error

If you believe we have published incorrect information, please contact us via the Share News form with the article title, the error, and a source for the correct information. We review all correction requests seriously.

Source Errors

If an error originates from a third-party source we reported on, we will note this clearly and update our article accordingly.
