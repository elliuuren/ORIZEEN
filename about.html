About ORIZEEN Times

ORIZEEN Times is an independent digital news platform committed to delivering timely, accurate, and balanced reporting on world events.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of informed citizens. Our mission is to make quality journalism accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Sources

ORIZEEN Times primarily relies on reputable external sources including international news agencies, government publications, and verified journalists. We always credit our sources and do not claim ownership of third-party content.

Editorial Standards

We strive for accuracy, fairness, and transparency in all our reporting. When errors occur, we correct them promptly. See our Corrections Policy for details.
